At Diesel Fuel Solutions, we believe that controlling fuel costs shouldn’t be complicated — it should be a strategic advantage. Founded in 2008 after more than a decade of working directly within the fuel industry, Diesel Fuel Solutions was built with one mission: to help carriers and fleets take control of their fuel expenses through education, smarter purchasing, and stronger partnerships.

Over the years, we saw firsthand how rising fuel costs and a lack of transparency hurt the carrier side of the trucking industry. We knew there had to be a better way — and we made it our purpose to deliver it. Today, Diesel Fuel Solutions partners with truck stops across the U.S. to provide our clients with direct access to lower fuel prices, customized fueling networks, and expert guidance to drive lasting savings.

EXPERTISE YOU CAN COUNT ON

Through our fuel consulting services, we help carriers build and maintain a network of preferred fueling stops, maximize fuel discounts, and eliminate wasteful spending. We also work directly with your drivers to educate them on where and how to fuel smarter, keeping savings high, fuel prices low, and your operational incentives firmly in place.

Introducing the CrossRoads Freight Card

A Smarter Way to Fuel

At the heart of our solutions is the CrossRoads Freight Card, a powerful tool designed to give fleets unmatched control, security, and savings. Accepted at over 16,000 truck stops and 95% of gas stations nationwide, the CrossRoads Freight Card is your all-in-one answer to rising fuel costs and administrative headaches.

With the CrossRoads Freight Card, you can:

  • Set custom purchase controls to prevent unauthorized spending
  • Capture unique driver IDs with every transaction for added security
  • Access real-time online account management for complete visibility
  • Take advantage of point-of-sale fuel discounts at major truck stop chains like TA, Petro, AMBEST, Sapp Bros., Kwik Trip, and more
  • Streamline your billing and eliminate the hassle of manual receipts

Whether you’re a small carrier fueling at gas stations or a large fleet stopping at truck plazas, the CrossRoads Freight Card is built to fit your needs — and grow with your business.
