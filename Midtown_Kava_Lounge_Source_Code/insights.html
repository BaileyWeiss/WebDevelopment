Join Us Watching the NFL Live Stream at Midtown Kava Lounge: Bills vs Dolphins

Published: January 2024

This upcoming Sunday, on January 7th, 2024, catch the exhilarating matchup between the Buffalo Bills and the Miami Dolphins at Midtown Kava Lounge. We're thrilled to announce that we'll be live-streaming the game for all our patrons to savor. Alcohol Alternatives in Tallahassee Florida

Published: October 2023

Memorial Day Fundraiser at Midtown Kava Lounge: Support A Soldier’s Child

Published: October 2023

Located in Tallahassee, Florida, Midtown Kava Lounge, owned by a dedicated disabled veteran, annually hosts a Memorial Day fundraiser that honors fallen heroes and extends a helping hand to their families. This event is a heartfelt celebration of service men and women's sacrifices, as well as a shining example of community support and unity. Learn about the impactful cause the fundraiser supports—A Soldier's Child, an organization that provides vital financial assistance to the children of fallen soldiers. Discover the meaningful symbolism of the Missing Man Table and the festivities that brought the community together at Midtown Kava Lounge's door.
