Memorial Day Fundraiser at Midtown Kava Lounge: Support A Soldier’s Child

Source: ABC 27 News

Located in Tallahassee, Florida, Midtown Kava Lounge, owned by a dedicated disabled veteran, has made it an annual tradition to host a Memorial Day fundraiser that celebrates the memory of fallen heroes and extends a helping hand to their families. This event not only commemorates the sacrifices of servicemen and women but also serves as a shining example of community support and unity.

A Soldier's Child: Where the Funds Go

The heart and soul of this fundraiser lie in the noble cause it supports. All the proceeds from the Memorial Day fundraiser are directed toward A Soldier's Child, a charitable organization with a vital mission. A Soldier's Child provides crucial financial assistance for scholarships, birthdays, and various other forms of support to the children of fallen soldiers, ensuring that their futures remain bright even in the face of adversity.

The 2023 MKL Memorial Day Fundraiser: Making Headlines

The most recent Memorial Day fundraiser held in May 2023 achieved significant recognition when ABC 27 News covered the event. The local news team was captivated by the heartwarming contributions of this community-driven initiative. Billy Rizzardi, the owner of Midtown Kava Lounge, shared his heartfelt motivation behind this yearly undertaking, stating, "Since I was getting a VA disability pension, I wanted to do something good with it." In addition to donating from his own pension, Rizzardi also contributes the funds received from his patrons on this special day.

The Missing Man Table: A Symbolic Tribute

As part of the event, Midtown Kava Lounge set up a poignant symbol of remembrance at the front of the bar—a Missing Man Table. This table remained cordoned off for 24 hours, marked by an upside-down glass and a rose in a vase, signifying the empty seats left behind by those who made the ultimate sacrifice. Patrons were encouraged not to sit at this table during Memorial Day but were welcomed to read the informative material that Rizzardi affixed to the wall, explaining the significance of this ceremony.

Community and Remembrance: MKL Festivities

In addition to the Missing Man Table, the outdoor patio of Midtown Kava Lounge was abuzz with activities that brought the community together. A spin-the-wheel game offered a chance to win exciting prizes, while a "Why is Memorial Day Important to You" board encouraged patrons to share their thoughts and feelings about this significant day.

A Deeper Connection: Memorial Day's Significance to Midtown Kava Lounge

Memorial Day holds a special place in the hearts of the Midtown Kava Lounge community. Billy Rizzardi, as a disabled veteran who served for over six years and was honorably discharged due to medical issues, understands the importance of commemorating the sacrifices of servicemen and women. Having faced challenges like PTSD and combat-related injuries, Rizzardi is on a mission to provide valuable information and support to fellow veterans, both in terms of physical and mental well-being.

The core value of community is deeply woven into the fabric of Midtown Kava Lounge. As Elizabeth Curley, the assistant manager, emphasized, "The direct action of taking care of your neighbors is something that is woven into this place from product to the daily activities and to the people who come here."

Midtown Kava Lounge: Where Tradition Meets Modernity
