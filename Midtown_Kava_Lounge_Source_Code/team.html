Billy Rizzardi

Owner

As the owner of Midtown Kava Lounge, it’s my obligation to keep my patrons happy and the business up and running. When I took over in 2020, I truly believed I could foster a strong community that would be similar to how I grew up. I was born and raised in Buffalo, New York where I grew up in an Italian/German neighborhood that deeply ingrained values of community and sharing. Being the third-generation American of my family, I wanted to carry out the legacy of the “American Dream” that my family gave everything for.

Upon assuming ownership of MKL, I aspired to transform the business's reputation, presenting it as an establishment genuinely dedicated to aiding individuals in their life goals. Since then, I am proud to say that we at MKL have helped to invest in small businesses, good causes, and good people.

In addition to my enthusiasm for community and business, finance has always captivated me. It was my favorite movie, The Wolf of Wall Street directed by Martin Scorsese, that initially sparked my interest in this realm. While the high-energy chaos of The Wolf of Wall Street might seem an unlikely source of inspiration, for me, it was a catalyst. Returning from a military deployment and grappling with unforeseen mental health challenges, the movie sparked a curiosity in me about financial markets, investing, and wealth creation. The underpinning narrative of harnessing control over one’s financial destiny interested me. I immersed myself in learning, devouring books like Rich Dad Poor Dad written by Robert Kiyosaki and Sharon Lechter, and The Intelligent Investor written by Benjamin Graham, to build a foundational understanding of money management and investment strategies. My journey saw me navigate through the volatile waves of Bitcoin and discover a more steady path with S&P 500 ETFs and thoughtful stock evaluations.

This personal odyssey within the realms of finance and business transcended mere monetary pursuits—it was a lifeline when I faced uncertainty regarding my health and future employability. These pursuits became more than hobbies; it was a means to forge my path and establish a semblance of stability amid the chaos.

Your journey with Midtown Kava Lounge is backed by this spirit. It’s more than a business; it’s a testament to perseverance, continuous learning, and the unwavering belief that we can navigate through challenges and carve out our destiny. Together, let’s embark on a journey where your financial dreams are not only valid but achievable.

Colby Fitzgerald

Manager / Kava Tender

My name is Colby and I am the Manager here at MKL. I’m currently working through my MS Ed.S in clinical mental health counseling at FSU. Besides my education, I am also really passionate about beach/water activities, traveling, and the Tampa Bay Lightning ice hockey team! MKL has provided me with a sense of community where I can be unapologetically myself and you can too–even if you’re a Florida Panthers fan, but don’t come at me saying they’re the best because I’m competitive. It’s very comforting to know that my kava family will always have my back and will always be willing to help me no matter what I’m going through.

If you want my opinion, the best drink at MKL is a tiki shot. Although tiki shots lose some points in the taste category, the feeling is amazing and honestly, I’ve managed the taste for the last two-ish years so– I believe in you! I can also promise you, if you want a tiki, you will not be the only one.

As the MKL manager, I’ve helped a lot through tough times at the bar and am very proud of all of the improvements and achievements we’ve been able to accomplish. I’m here to help you enjoy your stay with us, so don’t be shy to come talk to me. After all, you’ll know if I’m in the bar immediately if you see the big white fluffy Great Pyrenees sitting on the couch. Her name is China and while she is the mascot here at MKL, she’s also my beloved dog.
